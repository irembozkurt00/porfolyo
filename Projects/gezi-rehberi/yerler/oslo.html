Oslo: Fjords, Culture, and Nordic Charm



Oslo – Top Places to Visit

Oslo, the capital of Norway, is a city where modern architecture blends beautifully with stunning natural landscapes. Surrounded by forests, fjords, and mountains, Oslo offers visitors both cultural treasures and outdoor adventures. Here are some of the top places to explore:

  • Oslo Opera House

    A striking piece of modern architecture right on the waterfront. Visitors can even walk on its sloping roof for panoramic views of the city and fjord

  • Vigeland Sculpture Park

    Home to over 200 sculptures by Gustav Vigeland, this park is one of Oslo’s most iconic attractions.

  • The Viking Ship Museum

    Showcasing well-preserved Viking ships and artifacts, a must-see for history lovers.

  • Munch Museum

    Dedicated to Edvard Munch, the artist behind the world-famous painting The Scream.
